Private Pilot Certificate

Ready to experience the freedom of flight? Gibson Aviation's Private Pilot Certificate program is your gateway to the highway in the sky. Whether you're flying for fun, personal travel, or future career goals, this certification allows you to pilot single-engine aircraft across the U.S.

You will receive ground and flight training covering everything from aerodynamics and navigation to weather and aircraft systems. Under FAA Part 61, you will log a minimum of 40 flight hours, including solo and cross-country flights, and complete both a written exam and a check ride with an FAA examiner.
